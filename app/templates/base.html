iidx.fm


This web app will scrobble tracks played from your PW/PS profile to your Last.fm account.


• As this app is in beta, currently only Programmed Sun Omnimix is actively supported. Programmed World support will be implemented in the very near future - you can sign up in advance if you like.

• Tracks are scrobbled in batches, so it may take up to ~30 minutes before your tracks appear in Last.fm.

• Please make sure your player score history is public.

• Your Last.fm password is only used to generate a session key and is immediately discarded.
